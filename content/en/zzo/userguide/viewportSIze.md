If you want to increase your blog screen size, Just change the viewportSize value in the config.toml file.

```config.toml
...
viewportSize = "normal"
...
```

There are 5 options.

- widest: `1600px`
- wider: `1440px`
- wide: `1280px`
- normal: `960px`
- naroow: `800px`
